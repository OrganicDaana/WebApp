How AgriSwift Works

Simple steps to connect farmers with buyers efficiently

1. Register & Verify

Sign up as a farmer or buyer. Complete our simple verification process to ensure platform security and trust.

2. List Your Produce

Upload photos and details of your farm products. Set competitive prices based on our market analytics.

3. Connect & Deal

Buyers browse and connect directly with you. Negotiate terms and finalize deals through our secure platform.

4. Smart Delivery

Our logistics partners handle pickup and delivery. Track shipments in real-time through our mobile app.

5. Instant Payment

Receive payment immediately upon successful delivery. Multiple payment options available for your convenience.

6. Build Reputation

Earn ratings and reviews. Build your reputation to attract more buyers and command premium prices.
